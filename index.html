Honda Cars Dasmariñas

On June 19, 2008 , Honda Cars Dasmarinas (HCCV) proudly opened its doors to the residents of Cavite, marking a significant milestone in Honda's mission to provide unparalleled products and services directly to its valued customers. As the twenty-fourth dealer to join Honda's extensive network in the Philippines, HCCV stands as a testament to our commitment to accessibility and excellence.
